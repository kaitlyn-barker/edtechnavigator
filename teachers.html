Peer Reviews

Sarah M.
3rd Grade Teacher, Chicago, IL
ClassDojo

ClassDojo completely transformed how I communicate with parents. The instant messaging and behavior tracking have made parent-teacher relationships so much stronger. Students love earning points and it motivates them throughout the day.

James T.
7th Grade Math Teacher, Austin, TX
IXL Math

The diagnostic data from IXL is incredibly detailed. I can see exactly where each student is struggling at the skill level and assign targeted practice in minutes. My students' test scores improved noticeably within one semester of consistent use.

Rachel K.
2nd Grade Teacher, Portland, OR
Epic!

Epic! has been a game-changer for independent reading in my classroom. Students are genuinely excited to read during centers. The variety of books at different reading levels means every student finds something they love.

Marcus L.
10th Grade ELA Teacher, Atlanta, GA
Google Workspace for Education

Google Workspace has completely streamlined collaborative writing in my classroom. Students draft and revise essays in Google Docs, I leave inline comments in real time, and peer review is seamless. The integration with Google Classroom means I spend far less time on logistics and more on actual instruction.

Priya N.
5th Grade Science Teacher, Seattle, WA
Nearpod

Nearpod has made my science lessons interactive in ways I couldn't achieve with slides alone. The virtual field trips and 3D simulations genuinely captivate students, and the live poll and quiz features let me check understanding on the spot. Every student is engaged because they're all participating simultaneously on their own device.

DeShawn H.
Special Education Teacher, K–8, Denver, CO
Seesaw

Seesaw has been transformative for my students with IEPs. The multimodal submission options—voice recording, drawing, video—let every student demonstrate understanding in a way that works for them. The family communication feature is equally powerful; parents feel genuinely connected to their child's learning progress throughout the year.
