Enterprise Ed Tech Solutions

Schoology

Infrastructure District-wide Enterprise

Schoology is a powerful learning management system (LMS) that empowers school and district administrators to streamline curriculum delivery, assessment, and communication across all classrooms. Designed for scalability, Schoology gives administrators full visibility into instructional activity while supporting teachers and students with an intuitive, easy-to-use platform.

Key Features:

Curriculum Management Integration Ready Assessment Tools

Newsela

Instruction District-wide Enterprise

Newsela is a dynamic content platform that brings current events, primary sources, and informational texts into the classroom across all subject areas. With its unique text-leveling technology, Newsela allows administrators to support literacy school-wide while giving teachers the flexibility to meet every student where they are.

Key Features:

Differentiation Standards Alignment Assessment

Lexia Core5

Literacy K-12 Annual License

Lexia Core5 is a research-backed adaptive reading program that provides personalized literacy instruction for students in grades Pre-K–5. Built on the science of reading, Lexia Core5 helps administrators ensure every student receives targeted support to build strong foundational literacy skills, while giving teachers and leaders real-time data to drive intervention decisions.

Key Features:

Adaptive Learning Standards Alignment Progress Tracking

Key Considerations for Ed Tech Adoption

1

Align with Educational Goals

Ensure technology investments support your district's strategic academic objectives.

2

Prioritize Data Privacy

Verify FERPA compliance and establish clear data governance policies.

3

Plan for Professional Development

Budget adequate resources for training and ongoing teacher support.

4

Ensure Equity of Access

Address the digital divide by providing devices and connectivity for all students.

5

Establish Metrics for Success

Define clear KPIs to measure the impact of technology initiatives.
